Follow the latest news from euroAtlantic Airways.

euroAtlantic Airways CEO Eugénio Fernandes (photo: Expresso Newspaper)

Ten months after, together with other companies in the aviation sector, asked for support from the Government to face the impact of the very serious crisis caused by the Covid-19 pandemic, euroAtlantic remains unanswered. "I do not say that there is a check, but that there are decisions regarding support measures and targets", asks the president of euroAtlantic Airways, the largest Portuguese private aviation company, in an interview with Expresso. The manager considers the channeling of support exclusively for TAP to be unacceptable, leaving out private individuals. "In other countries it is not like that," he says. The Government, he argues, has been “very badly managing” the aviation dossier.

Jornal Expresso Article

The airline's president argues that the aviation sector dossier has not been well managed by the government. And criticizes the lack of transversal support for the sector.
